Title: Reading by the Light of Fireflies

Yin was diligent and tireless, learned and erudite. His family was impoverished and often could not afford lamp oil. In summer, he would fill a white silk bag with dozens of fireflies to illuminate his books, studying day and night.

Note: This story refers to the historical figure Che Yin (车胤) from the Eastern Jin Dynasty, known for his perseverance in studying under difficult conditions. The phrase "囊萤夜读" (náng yíng yè dú) has become a Chinese idiom symbolizing unwavering dedication to learning.

现代汉语译文：车胤勤奋好学，不知疲倦，他学识渊博，通晓多种学问。因家境贫寒，常常买不起灯油。到了夏天，他便用白绢做成袋子，装上几十只萤火虫，借着微弱的萤光读书，夜以继日地刻苦学习。

注释：故事出自《晋书·车胤传》，主人公车胤是东晋时期的名臣。成语“囊萤夜读”由此而来，用来形容在艰苦条件下坚持学习的毅力和勤奋精神，至今仍被广泛引用以激励学子。
